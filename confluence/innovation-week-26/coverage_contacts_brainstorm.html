Coverage and Contacts innovation ideas for banking CRM

Coverage and Contacts has two distinct failure modes in banking. Coverage is about which clients are assigned to which bankers — and the silent assumption that assignment equals relationship. Contacts is about who inside the client actually matters, who's been spoken to, and who's making decisions that your banker has never met. Both are broken in the same fundamental way: they are treated as administrative records, not as living intelligence.
